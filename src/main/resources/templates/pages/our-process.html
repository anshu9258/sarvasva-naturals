The Clinical Benchmark

Every batch undergoes a rigorous 5-step
validation protocol

01. Origin Mapping

Direct sourcing from heritage farms using blockchain to track soil health and crop rotation cycles.

02. Metal Screening

ICP-MS testing to ensure Lead, Arsenic, and Cadmium levels are significantly below FDA/FSSAI limits.

03. Microbial Purity

Strict screening for Salmonella, E. Coli, and Yeast/Mold using advanced PCR molecular diagnostics.

04. Bioactive Potency

We don't just test for safety; we measure the active therapeutic compounds like Curcumin and Piperine.

05. Digital Certification

Every single jar features a unique batch code linked to its specific laboratory analysis report, accessible instantly.
