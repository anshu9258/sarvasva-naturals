Our Journey

How We Got Here

2022
The Realisation
Our founder discovered that most commercial turmeric contains less than 2% curcumin — far below therapeutic levels. The idea for Sarvasva Naturals was born.
2023
Building the Supply Chain
We visited 40+ farms across Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Kerala and Meghalaya. Partnered with 8 heritage farms committed to regenerative practices.
2024
Lab Certification
Established partnerships with three ISO-certified independent laboratories for HPLC, ICP-MS and microbial testing on every batch.
2025
Launch
Sarvasva Naturals launched publicly. First batch of Rajapuri Curcumin Turmeric sold out in 72 hours. The purity movement began.
2026
Growing Strong
Expanding our catalogue with 8 lab-verified products. Trusted by doctors, nutritionists and thousands of health-conscious families across India.
